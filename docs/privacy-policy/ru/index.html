1.     Personal data processed

The Web-app does not require any personal information from users.

You may provide some personal information when you contact us for help, or you decide to create a profile on the Web-app. In this case, the following personal data will be processed:

-        Identifying data (first name, last name, e-mail address);

-        Information relating to your social network profile, if public and if you decide to log into the Web-app through social network applications and share your actions through the Web-app on those channels via the corresponding plug-ins (e.g., Facebook, Google, etc.). If you decide to share such information, personal data published on your social network profile will be collected by the Web-app and processed for the corresponding functions. Thus, the use of the said plug-in entails sharing the corresponding actions and information on the related social networks;

-        Your image in case you want to take a selfie after the game.

 

hereinafter commonly defined as “Personal Data”

 

In addition, the Web-app may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile unique device ID, your mobile operating system and language, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the Web-app (such as information about your game progresses and activity) and your coarse location. The Web-app may access certain device information and/or components automatically. This collection is only for the purpose of providing the services within the Web-app you choose to use. This information is not linked to personal information about the user.

Please note that certain sections of the Web-app will ask for access to parts of your device – namely, the camera, gyroscope and the storage. When you access these sections, a pop-up will ask you to give the Web-app permission to access the relevant parts of your device. You can adjust permissions given within the settings of your device itself. If you deny or remove this access, those sections of the Web-app may not work fully as intended.

Personal Data will be stored and deleted in accordance with Ferrero’s security policy for the duration necessary to achieve the purposes for which the Personal Data was collected and processed.
